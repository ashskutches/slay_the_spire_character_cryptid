# The Cryptid — Changelog

## v1.3.1 — Starting Deck & Card Rework

Redesigned the starting deck and revised several card mechanics to match the v1.3 reference.

### Starting deck
- **Old:** Probe ×5, Static Blast ×2, Strange Sighting ×1, Hide Evidence ×1, Abduction Beam ×1
- **New:** Probe ×4, Judgement ×4, Strange Sighting ×1, His Presence ×1

### New cards
- **Judgement** (Basic Attack) — Deal 5 damage; if enemy has Madness, deal 2 (5 upgraded) additional damage
- **His Presence** (Basic Attack) — Deal 10 (15) damage and apply Madness to target (all enemies when upgraded)

### Removed cards
- **Static Blast** — replaced by Judgement
- **Abduction Beam** — removed from card set

### Changed cards
- **Probe** — upgrade now draws 1 card (was +1 Paranormal)
- **Strange Sighting** — completely redesigned: consume 5 Paranormal, gain a random Entity orb and 5 Block (was draw + Paranormal)
- **Blackout** — upgrade now increases Weak (2→3) AND Paranormal (1→2); was Paranormal only
- **Eldritch Ritual** — upgrade now also increases Madness (2→4) in addition to adding draw

## v1.3.0 — Entity Overhaul

Complete redesign around the Entity orb system. Replaced the three-Influence archetype
(Gray/Reptilian/Nordic) with three channelable Entities (Gray Alien, Eldritch, Cryptid).
Added Madness as a combined Weak+Frail debuff. New starting relic: Tinfoil Skull.

### New mechanics
- Entity orb system: Gray Alien, Eldritch, Cryptid
- Madness debuff (counts as both Weak and Frail, stacks, decrements end of player turn)
- Starting relic Tinfoil Skull: gain 5 Paranormal on HP loss
- CryptidCharacter now starts with 3 orb slots

### New cards
- Crop Circle Pattern, Flashlight Sweep, Signal Booster, Scrambled Signal, Bad Feeling (Common)
- Black Eyed Children, Dimensional Shift, Cosmic Prayer, Eldritch Ritual (Uncommon/Power)
- Eldritch Transformation, Ancient Tablet (Rare/Power)
- Curses: Whispers, Night Terrors

### Removed cards
- Gray Visitation, Reptilian Agent, Nordic Visitor, Open Channel
- Signal Intercept, Roswell Incident, Hybrid Specimen, Peaceful Contact
- Astral Projection, False Memory, Dream Intervention, Global Blackout
- Mindwipe, Tinfoil Hat, They're Watching, Gray Alien Card, Crop Circles

### Changed cards
- Probe: now channels 1 Cryptid orb instead of just gaining Paranormal
- Abduction Beam: now channels 1 Gray Alien instead of applying Weak
- Missing Time: now applies Madness instead of Weak
- Men in Black: now removes debuffs from SELF instead of buffs from enemy
- Bigfoot: now a permanent power (deal Paranormal damage to random enemy each turn)
- Mothman: now reacts to Madness instead of spending Paranormal
- Observation Drone: now channels 1 random Entity per turn
- Conspiracy Board: every 3 Skills channels a random Entity (not just Paranormal)
- Paranormal Reactor: threshold raised from 3 to 6 Paranormal
- Glitch in the Sky: now gives 1 Energy next turn per Evoke (not Paranormal refund)
- Secret Meeting: now checks for at least 1 of each Entity orb in queue
