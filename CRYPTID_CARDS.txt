=========================================
THE CRYPTID — CARD REFERENCE v1.3
=========================================

CORE SYSTEM
-----------

PARANORMAL (Resource — Counter buff on player)
  At end of turn: gain Block equal to your FULL Paranormal stacks,
  then lose half of them (rounded down).
  Example: 6 Paranormal → gain 6 Block, left with 3.

ABDUCTED (Debuff — applied to enemies)
  Marks an enemy. Many cards trigger bonus effects on Abducted targets.
  Gray Alien orb evoke applies Abducted to ALL enemies.

MADNESS (Debuff — applied to enemies)
  Enemy deals 25% less damage AND gains 25% less Block.
  Combined Weak + Frail on a single power.
  Decrements at the end of the player's turn.
  Eldritch orb passive applies 1 Madness to a random enemy each turn.

ENTITY ORBS (3 types, slot into orb slots)

  GRAY ALIEN ORB
    Passive: Deal 3 damage to a random enemy.
    Evoke: Apply Abducted to ALL enemies. Deal damage to all enemies
           equal to the number of Gray Alien orbs channeled.

  ELDRITCH ORB
    Passive: Apply 1 Madness to a random enemy.
    Evoke: Consume ALL Madness from ALL enemies. Draw 1 card per Madness removed.

  CRYPTID ORB
    Passive: Gain 1 Paranormal.
    Evoke: Gain 1 Energy.
